  • Gameplay: child will use the arrow keys to select a shape
    then hit space to change what track is in focus
    hit the enter key when you want to check your solution

    you lose a life if you got it wrong
    or
    a fun anitmation plays when you win

    game is endless and you can keep track of how long you have been playing with the level or score counter (score is always +500 and level is +1)
