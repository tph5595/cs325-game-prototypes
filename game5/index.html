Chefin'

  • Originality: I modified a lot of the graphics from base templates i found online. The other originality that i added is that you have to spell out the food you want to serve the customers. thus combing learning with a fun game for kids.
  • Technical Merit: blind typing to get the food you want in a diner dash kinda game.
  • Prototype Postmortem:The hardest part was keeping track of what letter i am on and to calculate the closest customer
  • Prototype Assets: modified assets from online

  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Diner dash and learn to spell/type games for kids
  • High Concept: type to serve as many customers as you can in the given time
  • Theme: Sandwich
  • Mandated Variety: I have not made a game like this yet (diner dash)
  • Prototype Goal: see if kids enjoy the game or if it is too hard for them
  • Player Experience Goals: Learn new words while having fun
  • Gameplay: players ask customers what they want to have with SPACEBAR.
    Then they go over the bar and type the name of that item
    finally they go back to the customer and deliver the food with the ENTER Key
  • Strategies: type fast and dont forget
  • Story/Setting/Premise: you are a bartender serving customers. as many as you can until the store closes for the night
  • Target Audience:Kids who are learning to type and spell
  • Play Time: 150 seconds
