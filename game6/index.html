  • Player Experience Goals: Competative game, addrenlaline.
  • Gameplay: Player sees a multiplication problem (number of nuts) appear on the screen.
    They guess what the answer was with the arrow keys (up and down to +/- 1, left and right to -/+ 10)
    They hit SPACEBAR to lock in answer
    Squirrels estimate how close you were (more the better, 10 max)
    You then get one more guess (Same mechanic as before for selecting and locking in)
    The real answer is shown and points are given (more points for closer guesses, - points for over 50 away)
    Hit ENTER to move to next round

    0 points -> Lose
    1000 points -> Win
  • Strategies: Look fast and dont forget
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Help the squirrels prepare for the winter by collecting enough nuts to survive.
  • Target Audience:Kids who love fast paced games and want to get better at math estimations
  • Play Time: 120 seconds
